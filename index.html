Welcome To KidKalc

Our goal at KidKalc is for any kid to have fun while learning. So, we created this fun, fast and engaging website for kids to test their mathematical skills.
First, choose your desired grade, and simply start answering the randomly generated questions to earn points.
Once you have answered 15 questions, you will get one try at our game. The game is a fun, skill-based tetris-like game.
Your scores will be saved locally if you don't sign in/up. However, if you do sign in/up, you will be able to access your scores and achievements from any device.
Built for the Innovative Website 2020 Competition.
