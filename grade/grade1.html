Scoring System


If either the shadow of the word "difficulty", or the shadow of the actual question turns a light green. This indicates a easy difficulty question. Easy questions are worth 1 point.

If either the shadow of the word "difficulty", or the shadow of the actual question turns yellow. This indicates a medium difficulty question. Mediocre questions are worth 2 points.

If either the shadow of the word "difficulty", or the shadow of the actual question turns a light red. This indicates a hard difficulty question. Hard questions are worth 3 points.
You will also lose 2 points for every incorrect answer regardless of question difficulty.
